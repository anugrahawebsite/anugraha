Volunteers

Madhuri Kelkar

Senior Teacher

I volunteer at Anugraha on a daily basis. I help teach the members to make the various products and assist them in production. I am primarily concerned with the manufacture of decorative items like ‘rakhis’ and ‘torans’. I also put my creative juices to use to create vivid designs for the same.

Pam Gandhekar

Occupational Therapist

I volunteer with all the young people to improve their hand skills and visual perception skills, both of which are required for the various vocational tasks they participate in. I also tailor the occupational therapy activities to suit the young person’s cognitive level and thereby increase their self confidence.

Samidha Deshpande

Product Sales

I volunteer to work with special hands at Anugraha few hours every week and market the products manufactured at Anugraha.
